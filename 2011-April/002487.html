Hi Mohsen,

> Thanks for the reply; it helped me to figure out how/when the IPSEC SA are
> generated. I still need to figure out how/where the CK(confidentiality
key)
> and IK (integrity key) are generated. My understanding is that CK and IK
are
> generated by SCSCF and put into the www-authenticate header in response
> to the initial register from the UE and passed to PCSCF who then takes
them
> out of the header and use them to create the IPSEC SA. 

No, that's not correct. CI and CK are generated by the HSS and transferred
within the authentication vector to S-CSCF in the diameter MAA request.
Just look into that message ("SIP-Auth-Data-Item" AVP).
The reason for this is that only the HSS knows the secret key and that key
is never transferred.

The rest of your view is correct (p-CSCF extracts CI 6 CK).
