> Is there any way to define trusted entity for OpenIMS ? 

Yes. The concept of trusted domains (TS 24.229 §4.4)  is implemented -
AFAIK.
You can define trusted domains in I-CSCF and if a domain is untrusted
specific header fields are stripped off before forwarding or not accepted if
received. Look for the "NDS" functions.
With a specific config-file for I-CSCF (icscf.thig.cfg) you also can
implement topology hiding (encryption of specific header fields).

I hope that helps.
