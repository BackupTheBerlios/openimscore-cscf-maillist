Hi Mohsen,

> Thanks for the reply. as you mentioned , UE sends all requests to the
P-CSCF
> regardless of the route set. Please find enclosed 2 log files; one
includes
> messages exchanged between UE and P-CSCF, for simplicity I have omitted
> the re-registrations and notifies, just wanted to show that P-CSCF rejects
the
> re-SUBSCRIBE. And the other one includes the display logs from P-CSCF and
> S-CSCF.

The situation is clear when you look at the Route header field in the second
SUBSCRIBE request.
It contains 2 Route header fields:
	Route: <sip:mo at pcscf.open-ims.test:4060;lr> 
	Route: <sip:orig at scscf.open-ims.test:6060;lr>

But the previously received 200 OK to the SUBSCRIBE request contains only
one Record-Route header field:
	Record-Route: <sip:mo at pcscf.open-ims.test:4060;lr>
This is the dialog route for the SUBSCRIBE initiated dialog which the P-CSCF
checks.

Therefore the P-CSCF recognizes that the UE does not honor the dialog route:
	SIP/2.0 400 Bad Request - Not following indicated dialog routes

 Just omit the 2nd Route header field and it will work..
