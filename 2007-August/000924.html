Yes, unfortunately it does not appear in the specs as clearly as perhaps it
should be.

These parameters are added as "Service Information" data as part of the
evaluation of the Filter Criteria. Check 23.218, section 5.2 and 6.9.2.5.

Basically what you do is: If (rest of filter criteria) and the session is
originating then add ServiceInformation = "whatever your AS needs to
identify originating sessions".

Similar for terminating, unregistered user, etc.

Hope it helps,

Jose
