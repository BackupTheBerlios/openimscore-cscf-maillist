Hi,

I just tested I-CSCF-THIG and discovered the following issue:

In case of roaming and an I-CSCF-THIG is involved two Service-Route header
fields are created by the S-CSCF (one for "orig at scscf" and an additional for
"thig at icscf"). 

I think this is a bug. One Service-Route should be sufficient.
As can further be seen only one Service-Route is enforced by the P-CSCF.

I have enclosed the tcpdump-files and SIP-scenario traces for both networks
(net1.test and net2.test).
The traces cover the following steps:
- registration of sip:alice at net2.test in net1
- registration of sip:bob at net1.test in net2
- sip:bob at net1.test invites sip:alice at net2.test

cheers
-franz
