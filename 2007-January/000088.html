Franz Edler wrote:
> Hi,
>
> I just found that the P-CSCF does not re-use the initial From-tag on
> re-subscription to the reg-event. The Call-Id is the same one but the
> From-tag is a new one and therefore it is not the same dialog as requested
> by RFC 3265.
>
> See the attached Wireshark trace-file.
> - Initial subscription to reg-event of Bob in packet 32
> - Re-subscription to reg-event of Bob in packet 106
> - Initial subscription to reg-event of Alice in packet 89
> - Re-subscription to reg-event of Alice in packet 135
>
> I think that's a bug.
