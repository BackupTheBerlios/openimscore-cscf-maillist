Hi,

An issue I would like to get confirmed is the missing encryption of
Record-Route and Route header fields. This has still to be done, isn't it?

As you can see in SIP Message F33/Packet 72 of the trace in net1 (INVITE
arriving at Alice) the Via headers are encrypted but the Record-Route header
fields are not.
And also SIP Message F47/Packet 91 of the trace in net2 (ACK being sent by
Bob) the Route Header fields are not encrypted.

According to
- TS 24.228/Table 17.4.2.1-5 "INVITE (I-CSCF to P-CSCF)"
- TS 33.203 §6.4 Hiding mechanisms
those headers should be encrypted for topology hiding.

Cheers
Franz
