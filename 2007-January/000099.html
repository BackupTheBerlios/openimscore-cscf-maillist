From a short look on your trace I can see some issues with your platform
configuration:

What is your network architecture? Please clarify?

Did you change the default port numbers of Open Source IMS?

What is your P-CSCF, port 5060? I don't think so. 
Port 5060 seems to be the I-CSCF and this is where the UE sends its INVITE.
That's not correct. You should send it towards P-CSCF.

Why does your client include "P-Asserted-Identity"?

Your client does not honour the Service Route received during registration. 
There is no Route header field included.
