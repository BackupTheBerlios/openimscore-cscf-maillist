Hi dear openImsCore people,
I am not 100% sure, but it looks like there are some issues with registering over ipsec; in fact I have spent sometime to look at the traces of P-CSCF ; and it looks that P-CSCF never updates the reg_state of the contact when the registratio is done over ipsec. The registration goes successful, so my UE receives 200OK and HSS shows that the UE is indeed registered; however the P-CSCF believes that it is not and rejects the SUBSCRIBE coming over the same security association. P-CSCF indeed subscribe with S-CSCF and does receive the notification about the registration state, but in the code it looks that in the registrar_subscribe.c , it only tries to call update_r_contact and it can not find it , so the contact reg_state stays PENDING all the time. I am wondering why P-CSCF does not also call the update_r_contact_sec as this seems to be responsible for secure registration state update. The update_r_contact_sec does get invoked by save_contact_security
 function in the security.c , but save_contact_security function sets the reg_state only to PENDING.
your help is greatly appreciated.
