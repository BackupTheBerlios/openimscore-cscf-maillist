Raphael,

Your AS is not receiving the same INVITE twice: first one is in the
origination leg, the second one is in the terminating leg. S-CSCF
marks the Record-Route signalling it: sip:mo at scscf.open-ims.test vs
sip:mt at scscf.open-ims.test.

If you would like to be involved just in one of the legs (originating
or terminating), setup the iFC accordingly.

Jose
