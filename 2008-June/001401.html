Hi Raphael,

> Finally, I guess the change of the trigger isn't the good solution, indeed
> the following responses continue to be forward to AS. 

You cannot avoid that. Responses always take the same route back as the
request.

> ... But IMS
> continues to forward all the messages of the conversation to the AS.
> I'd like to find a way to stop this behavior.

It is the second trigger point which causes the INVITE request to be routed
to Mobicents again. When you compare the first INVITE with the second you
can see that the second INVITE additionally contains the Route header
"Record-Route: <sip:mt at scscf.open-ims.test:6060;lr>". This shows that now
the trigger matches at the terminating half call.

I suggest you should qualify the trigger point with an appropriate session
case (Origin -Session) to avoid a second trigger at the terminating half
call.
