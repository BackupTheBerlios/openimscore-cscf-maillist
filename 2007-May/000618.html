Q1 - No, your recognition is not right. The I-CSCF uses ALSO the S-CSCF
name received, if any. A list is compiled and that value should be on
the first position.

Of course that it checks the result-code of the operation and then it
only proceeds on successful ones.

Q4 - No, your assertion is wrong. The S-CSCF name doest not have
