> In the sequence of tests with our AS’s I found that “isc_failure”
> function was considering 408 and 480 responses as failures to contact
> the AS, which is not in accordance with 24.229, since the
> specification says “If the S-CSCF fails to receive a SIP response or
> receives a 408 (Request Timeout) response or a 5xx response from the
> AS, the S-CSCF shall:…”.
>
>  
>
> In my case I switched the following line:
>
> if (ps->code!=408 && ps->code!=480) {
>
> with this one:
>
> if (ps->code!=408 && (ps->code/100!=5)){
>
