> I have tested under Proxy time-out situation.

I also have done now - in my own interest.
I could not reproduce any issue. I have tested two cases

1. Alice only answers with 180 Ringing (trace: osi26105-net1)
2. Alice does not even answer (trace: osi26106-net1)

In both cases S-CSCF-term sends 408 timeout to S-CSCF-orig. So the flow is
o.k. (see the scenarios attached).

The problem in your scenario is that you send CANCEL directly to S-CSCF,
that's not allowed in IMS, and in addition CANCEL is never sent end-to-end
(also in pure SIP world). CANCEL is a hop-by-hop request.
