Still I fail to see the use of those SAs even if you could set them up
with just half of the information (other than having a way of doing a
DoS attack on the P-CSCF ;-) ). The 3GPP specs ask that the only
acceptable unencrypted messages would be the REGISTER requests.

If you want them created, please provide a Security-Client header in the
REGISTER message! If not, the OpenIMSCore would suppose that you do not
support any security technology.

-Dragos
