Hello Phani,

But it doesn't make any sense to have just half of the SAs... Then you
won't be able to talk to the P-CSCF at all as it would wait for
encrypted messages and SIPp will not encrypt them, or the P-CSCF will
send them encrypted and SIPp won't be able to decrypt them...

The behavior of the P-CSCF is dynamic based on the presence/absence of
the Security-Client headers.

-Dragos
