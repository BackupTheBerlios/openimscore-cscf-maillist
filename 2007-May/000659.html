What revision of the OpenIMSCore are you using? Recently I identified a
possible bug in handling of the SUBSCRIBE dialogs that could cause the
premature expiration of the respective dialogs (r281). This should be
exactly the errors that you would get - one for the P and the other from
the S. However, I am not sure that I fixed or broke it :(... So please
tell me your revision and in case that it is <r281 please upgrade.
