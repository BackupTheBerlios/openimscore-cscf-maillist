Hello Choi,

the REGISTER request represents the client's wishes and not what
actually was accepted by a S-CSCF registrar. For one example, the
expiration of the contacts in the REGISTER can be changed by the S-CSCF
and reduced to what the network wants, so you can not really use in the
P-CSCF the contacts in the REGISTER requests.

Cheers,
-Dragos
