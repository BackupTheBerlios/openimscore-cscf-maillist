Hi Min,

The persistency modules have been developed inside the Open IMS Core 
project, while the tm entirely into SER. Saving the tm contents was out 
of our scopes back then, but if anyone would find the time and resources 
to also save/retrieve the tm it would be certainly an advantage. But 
typically, we believe that this is not such a critical issue, as most 
transactions would be recovered by simple retransmissions.

However, the issue that you discovered is quite bad. I think that unless 
new routines would be developed for tm, could the p_dialog references to 
tm be blanked or somehow masked, so that we won't have crashes?

Cheers,
-Dragos
