> Thus, the error points exactly to the "3" in the name and indeed, if I
> remove the "3" from the name (and reconfigure everything else
> accordingly), it works!

This is according to the definition of labels in DNS. See at RFC 1035: "The
labels must follow the rules for ARPANET host names.  They must start with a
letter, end with a letter or digit, and have as interior characters only
letters, digits, and hyphen.  There are also some restrictions on the
length.  Labels must be 63 characters or less."

It is not allowed to start a label with a digit.
So you have to find another solution for your domain configuration.
