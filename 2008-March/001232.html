> The thing is that I need to register my MRF, in some way i don't know,
> in the core so that SCSCF lets my request continue and forwards it,
> because i'm in a termintating situation and according to scscf.cfg my
> request-URI must be "sip:(.*)@open-ims\.test(.*)".
>   
only clients should register and not core components. You probably have 
a way to tell if a request is for an MRF. Then look at how the PSTN 
Gateway is configured and used in scscf.cfg. You need something similar, 
that will trigger on the specific MRF URI and just fwd the request to 
the MRF's contact...
> Is there any option to register my MRF in the Openimscore to get it
> named as "mrf at open-ims.test"? which one? Any help to do it?
>   
you can also have a PSI... but for MRFs, I would rather skip the iFC 
triggering and hard-code the redirect it in the scscf.cfg. In the end, 
the MRF is a kind of operator configuration issue.

Also you can have a PSI that redirects in I-CSCF mode - that is when 
somebody tries to find mrf at open-ims.test through open-ims.test's I-CSCF, 
the HSS will respond to the LIR with an S-CSCF AVP that is actually the 
MRF's contact. But then no S-CSCF will be involved in the signaling 
path... if you don't mind, then it's OK.
