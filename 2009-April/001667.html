> Team,
>
> To remove duplicates you could post process the captured file using the
> wireshark sub-application. The tool is called editcap.
>
> With this you can remove the duplicates :
>
>    # editcap  -d    <infile.pcap>   <outfile.pcap>
>
> Depending on the way you collected all the traffic it may be the case that
> your SIP messages have different mac-addresses while the contents (payload)
> is the same. Then editcap will not treat it as equal. in that case you may
> want to use the wireshark filtering techniques to get them removed.
>
> Cheers,
>
>        - Dick Gooris
