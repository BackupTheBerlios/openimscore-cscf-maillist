Are you sure that the SUBSCRIBE is sent to the P-CSCF? You should only
add the Service-Route as Route and optional, on top, the P-CSCF, but not
the Path header.

What revision are you using? A network trace would be of great help (use
wireshark/ethereal for example to capture the traffic).
