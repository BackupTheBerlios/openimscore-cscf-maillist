If you get a server name AVP from the HSS, then the I-CSCF will forward
there. If you don't, like in the initial registration, then the I-CSCF
will compile a ordered list of S-CSCF, based on the capabilities
received for that user from the HSS, that will best match for that user.
In order to do this selection, the I-CSCF has to know the
"configuration" of the home network - which S-CSCFs are available and
what are they capable of.

-Dragos
