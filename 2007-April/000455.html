Hello Stephan,

1. Could you please send a network trace or something that would allow
me to easily replicate the issue?

2. This is a feature. The 401 should not relay the cypher and integrity
keys to the client, especially over unsecured transport. Then what is
the value of CK and IK if they are disclosed and anybody eaves-dropping
can decode the encoded traffic? The UE should compute the CK and IK from
the random in the nonce and the secret key.

3. Please check if your DNS is updated accordingly. The P-CSCF does a
DNS query for the Request-URI domain to find the right I-CSCF.

-Dragos
