no, at the moment it does not. It is of course of many people's 
wish-list, but I don't know anyone that is working on this now.

Also with this would be the support for caller preferences which might 
be an alternattive for your problem. The S-CSCF now saves the Contact 
header parameters, but the header matching functionality is not there 
either.

So of course, any contributions are welcomed and if you require help 
please let me know.
