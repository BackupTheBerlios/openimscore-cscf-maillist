After a quick look at your trace the problem is clear: Your AS does not
route correctly (see attached scenario).
The INVITE sent to the AS (packet 1283) contains two Route header fields
included by the S-CSCF, but The AS does not
- eliminate the first Route header field
- forward the request to the SIP URI of the second remaining Route header
field.

Instead the AS keeps the Route header fields untouched and sends the request
to the I-CSCF.
