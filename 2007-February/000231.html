Hello Heiko,
 
> I think the home S-CSCF should remove the P-ACCESS-NETWORK-INFO header
> by forwarding the message, but in my traces the header is still exists
> in the forwarded message.

That's o.k. as long as the forwarded-to-node is trusted. 3GPP TS24.229 says:
"For the purpose of the P-Access-Network-Info header, a trust domain also
applies. This trust domain is identical to that of the P-Asserted-Identity."
