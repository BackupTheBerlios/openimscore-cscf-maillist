> Regexes are supported within the configuration files
> which gives
> you a certain flexibility in match cases. You can
> replace a domain name:
> uri=~"sip:(.*)@open-ims\.test(.*)"
> with
> uri=~"sip:(.*)@my\.new\.test\.env"
> 
> In case you want to do it by hand, just be sure that
> you worked through
> the whole cfg file. Or you can just let a
> script(configurator.sh) do it for you.
> Check therefore the ser_ims/trunk/cfg directory.
> 
> I dont think that scscf is unpredictable if it is
> configured properly.
> 
> hope this helps,
> Umut
