The 01_persistency branch is since r129 complete from the file
snapshooting point of view. So if you want to give it a try, would
appreciate any feedback before I will merge it into the main tree.

What id does: every <configurable> seconds, the state data is dumped
into files. Also, when you stop the P/S-CSCF nicely, a snapshot is made.
On start-up, the last snapshots are loaded. I have tested it a little
and restarting the CSCFs doesn't seem to have much effect anymore on the
registered users.

What is saved:
P-CSCF:
    - the registrar
    - the dialogs
    - the subscription to reg

S-CSCF
    - the registrar
    - the dialogs
    - the authentication vectors

-Dragos

>
