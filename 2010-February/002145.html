Hello Min,

In the past the ports of the flows were ignored and now they are also
set/sent in the ipfilerrule. This is the big change :d.

The old version was working with the UCT PCRF because there were no
ports set/sent.
So, there are 2 options: 
a)use the PCSCF with the ports disabled, and you will have the backwards
compatibility 
or 
b)use the functionality with the ports enabled and modify yourself the
UCT PCRF or ask them nicely to do this (if they still have interest in
this). 

I have tested the configuration with and without ports and the pcscf
does not crash. But as I am using another pcrf I am not able to test the
backwards compatibility. Could you test it please. It would be important
to know.
