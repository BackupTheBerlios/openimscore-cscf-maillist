Hi Raphael,

> I try to transfer a call, but when one the client send a REFER to the
> other, OpenIMS doesn't recognize the user who is trying to be joined and
> send a 403 - Forbidden Not Registered! You must register first  with a S-
> CSCF. The client which sends the REFER is IMS Communicator (if it can be
> useful for you guys).

I found the following:
The REFER is rejected by the P-CSCF at the terminating side, because it does
not identify the request as a terminating one. According to the code the
P-CSCF checks the first Route header for the terminating indication and
cannot find it.

Unfortunately the tracefile is not complete. The internal messages between
S-CSCF and P-CSCF are missing. Therefore the REFER request which causes the
rejection and the 403 response is not shown (should be between packet 104
and 105).

Maybe you can complete the trace-file or as an alternative: look at the
logfile of P-CSCF and increase the debug level.
