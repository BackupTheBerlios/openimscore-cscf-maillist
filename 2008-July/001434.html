Hi,

When using the 08_cdp_sessions branch with 2 domains I've found that the
orig_realm and terminating realm AVPs in the AAR messages created by the
P-CSCF in each realm are not  correct.

I have 2 clients running in 2 different domains (e.g. dom1, dom2).  When
I setup a session across domains from dom1 to dom2 - the AAR messages
created by the P-CSCF's in each domain should list dom1 as the
originating realm and dom2 as the terminating realm.  Instead the P-CSCF
puts the domain of the forced QoS peer (The PCRF) as both originating
and terminating realms (i.e. P-CSCF in dom1 has dom1 as terminating and
originating realm, while P-CSCF has dom2 as terminating and originating
realm).
