Hello Sathya,
 
What I have advised you to do is to do an svn update on the ser_ims folder, that will update the cfg/pcscf.cfg file.
Then you have to copy the new version of the pcscf.cfg to /opt/OpenIMSCore and run the P-CSCF again.
 
As another advise it would be great just to tell us what svn version of the project you are using, so that we can have a look at a file/module, not attach it to the e-mail.
To find the svn version, use the command svn info.
 
There is more to OpenIMSCore than the openimscore.org site. Have a look at http://svn.berlios.de/viewcvs/openimscore/.
 
If you were wondering what was the bug, go to http://svn.berlios.de/viewcvs/openimscore/ser_ims/branches/12_em_services/cfg/pcscf.cfg?r1=720&r2=735 .
 
Best regards,
Ancuta
