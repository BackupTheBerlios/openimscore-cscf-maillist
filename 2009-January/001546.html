Hi Siddu,

> I am getting "400 bad request-Not following indicated service routes".
> Please suggest possible solution.

I guess that your client is not an IMS client (supporting e.g the Service
Route header field). That' s no problem with OpenIMSCore, you can adapt
the situation in "pcscf.cfg" file. You simply have to enable Variant #2
and set Variant #1 under comment.

----- snip of pcscf.cfg
--------------------------------------------------------

	if (!P_follows_service_routes()){
		#Variant 1 - deny access to the network
		sl_send_reply("400","Bad Request - Not following indicated
Service-Routes");
		break;
		#Variant 2 - enforce routes and let the dialog continue
   		#P_enforce_service_routes();
	}
