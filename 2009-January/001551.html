> Attaching the tcpdump capture.

I found a strange situation in your trace:
When the INVITE is forwarded from S-CSCF (originating half-call) to the
S-CSCF (terminating half-call) the Route header field is not removed. This
should be done by "loose_route()" function in "route(Orig)". As a result the
next hop (S-CSCF terminating half-call) considers the INVITE request again
as an originating one ...

As a next step it could be helpful to see an S-CSCF log with increased debug
level to see why this happens.
