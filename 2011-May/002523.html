I identified a problem: Your client does not include an "ealg" parameter in
the Security-Client header field. According to RFC 3329 no encryption should
be used in that case (confirmed by P-CSCF with " ealg=null;"), but your
client encrypts the SUBSCRIBE request. The P-CSCF therefore rejects the
gibberish.
 I assume that packet 470 contains the encrypted SUBSCRIBE and packet 681
the encrypted SUBSCRIBE response. Packets 682 and 683 are a repetition I
assume.

I think that should be corrected.

@Daniel: When encryption is used it is not easy to identify the packets in
wireshark!
