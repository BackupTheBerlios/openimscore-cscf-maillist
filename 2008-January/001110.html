So that would be a knoppix. It seems that strangely your system does not
know about the EPERM error for the kill() function. Strange as I did not
ever saw this problem again. Also, I can't really debug it as I need the
same system. Neither have I seen such a report for knoppix.

You could either:
- try to find why EPERM does not exist on your system. On mine, the man
page (man 2 kill) mentions it.
- try another distribution
- just comment the "|| errno==EPERM" part.
