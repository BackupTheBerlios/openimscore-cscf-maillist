>
> This case is covered by TS 23.228 §4.3.1:
> When a UE is registered in the IM CN Subsystem with an IP address, any
> change to this IP address that is used to access the IM CN subsystem will
> result in dropping the active SIP dialogs, and shall trigger automatic
> registration.
>
> In your case (OpenSourceIMS) the problem is in the transport layer of the
> access network. In an ideal case the drop of a security association is
> recognised by the P-CSCF and as a consequence the active call, the dialog
> and also registration state are dropped. If the P-CSCF does not recognise
> the lost connection and drop these data it leads to the mentioned behaviour.
>
