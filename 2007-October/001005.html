As discussed here
https://lists.berlios.de/pipermail/openimscore-users/2007-October/004240...
, if the client misbehaves and sends different Contacts in the 2
REGISTER messages, the IPSec contact info is saved from the first
REGISTER. This info should be update before setting the remaining 3 SAs,
in the 2nd REGISTER, as this is the only authorized and final message
containing the Contact address.
