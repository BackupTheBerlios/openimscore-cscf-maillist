Hello Arvind,

By default, Wireshark decodes Diameter only when one end is on port
3868. Because we are using 3 peers on just one host, the I-CSCF/S-CSCF
run on 3869/3870. If they will connect first to the HSS, you will see
the traffic on 3868, but in your trace, I think that the HSS initiated
the connection.

To decode a message as Diameter - click-right on it - Decode-as - select
Diameter

The UAA containing the Unable to comply response is in packet 41 in your
trace.

Cheers,
-Dragos
