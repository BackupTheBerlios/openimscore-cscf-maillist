> When you do "tel:+1234567" -> "sip:user_public at open_ims.test",
> how do you know the "open_ims.test" part? I ask because it is
> not contained in the original tel uri...

I think you have to look at the service profile, in the same way it is
already done for "sip:user_public at open-ims.test" -> "tel:+1234567".

But, as JF pointed out, I think that I missed a point when I read
24.229.
The spec does not require "tel:+1234567" ->
"sip:user_public at open-ims.test", it is "tel:+1234567" ->
"sip:+1234567 at open-ims.test". However, same problem here: In order to
obtain the domain you will still have to use the service profile.
