Hi Eivar,

could you please post the scscf.cfg and the scscf output around the 
registration? It seems that the Early-IMS authentication went fine, but 
then there was an error in the server assignment, on the S-CSCF, after 
the SAR/SAA was successful.

BTW: - you might want to put a real Contact address in the REGISTER, 
containing the IP and port of the client... Also, there's no Expires 
header or expires parameter to the contact. This might have caused the 
failure as the S-CSCF might have not properly detected if you do a 
registration or de-registration.
