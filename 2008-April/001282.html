Hi Diego,

> Due to our application not allow us to configure the proxy port, it is
> sending the Register message to 5060 port. Because of this we changed the
> P-CSCF port to 5060 and the I-CSCF port to 4060 in pcscf.cfg, icscf.cfg
> and icscf.thig.cfg files.

In this case you have also to modify your DNS data.
The SIP service for the domain (interrogating the HSS and forwarding to an
S-CSCF) is offered by the I-CSCF. The SRV record in your DNS must point to
port 4060.

For DNS details see: http://www.openimscore.org/installation_guide#annexa
