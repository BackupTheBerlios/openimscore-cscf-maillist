Hi Carsten,

I may be incorrect but my understanding (from the 3gpp TS's) is that the CDF
is used ONLY for offline charging (post paid, etc). The spec also says that
for offline charging the sCSCF MAY talk directly to CDF. For online charging
however (OCS), the spec seems to mandate that the sCSCF MAY only communicate
via IMS GWF.

from my perspective, I would imagine that there is still considerable value
in having the Ro interface built into sCSCF?

Cheers
Jason
