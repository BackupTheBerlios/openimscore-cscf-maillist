Hello Naga,

we are confident to have such UAR/UAA messages, please trace for the
relevant TCP ports (3868 - 3870 per default) to see them e.g. in
Wireshark.

For the difference in between the two pairs: the first one is there to
determine User Authorisation and to make a selection of S-CSCFs
possible, the second one is mainly used for routing the message to the
S-CSCF already assigned for registration. TS 29.228 shows the details.

- Peter
