Fixed since r202. There were 2 bugs related to  timed-out deregistration:
- the dialog for the subscription was dropped before the notification -
created a failure in notify or a crash
- the SAR function was always returning FALSE when called internally
after the last revamp of that code to support individual contact updates.

Now it should be ok...
