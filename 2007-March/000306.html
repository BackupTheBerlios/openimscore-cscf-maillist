Hello Srini Vasan,

the reason why they can not interconnect i|s-cscf with
hss lies within your dns resolution. Starting with ping 
operation is a good step. If that fails, it is a good idea  
to check the dns settings.

A part of the Installation-Guide at our website is
dedicated to this problem. So i think this would help
you solve your problems.:
http://openimscore.org/docs/install.html#annexa

Greetz,
Umut
