Hi Ali,

I use the following commands in MySQL:

use hssdb;
update impi set auth_scheme='Digest-DM5' where impi_id=1; (for alice)
update impi set algorithm='MD5' where impi_id=1;

You may leave where statement optionally.

Re-registration or restart of IMS may be necessary (I don't remember).
